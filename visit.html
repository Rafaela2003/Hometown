Culture and Entertainment

Faro has an official holiday on 7th September due to a festival hosted by students from the University of Algarve. Additionally Faro's motorcycle club is responsible for the largest motorcycle events in Portugal and Europe. Estadio Algarve is the football stadium shared by Faro and Loule and it was the venue for the 2004 Euro's.


Ria Formosa

The Ria Formosa is a lagoon that acts as a system of barrier islands that connects to the sea through six inlets.

Igreja de Santa Maria

A Roman Catholic cathedral that has been the seat of the Diocese of Faro since 1540.

Muncipial Museum

A historical convent that has been turned into a museum and features permanent and temporary archeological exhibitions.
